# Noun

## ròcho

1. A furious rage or anger.
2. A screaming fighting.

## rócho

1. A long-term anger.

# Verb

## ròcho

1. To have a screaming fight.
2. To fight for the sake of fighting.

## rócho

1. To have a passive-aggressive fight.
2. To fight without revealing the purpose of the fight.
